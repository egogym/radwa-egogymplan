Radwa Weight Loss Program

4-Week Comprehensive Weight Loss and Strength Training Plan

Program Overview

This 4-week weight loss program combines strength training and cardio for optimal fat loss results. Training days are Saturday, Sunday, Monday, and Wednesday, with built-in recovery days. Each session includes a proper warm-up and cool-down to prevent injury and promote recovery.

Training Guidelines

Key Points

  • Rest 30-60 seconds between exercises
  • Use Tuesday and Friday for recovery
  • Focus on proper form over weight/speed
  • Stay consistent with the schedule

Nutrition & Recovery

  • Maintain a caloric deficit for weight loss
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • Get 7-8 hours of sleep per night
  • Consider light stretching on rest days
